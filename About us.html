About Peaceful Restaurant


The Peaceful Restaurant is Canada’s leader in the premium mandarin cuisine. We serve our guests with well-known northern Chinese dishes, such as Kung Pao Chicken, Xi'an White Lamb Stew, Mandarin Wonton Soup, Sichuan Broth Braised Fish, and Xing-Jiang Cumin Lamb. We freshly handmake both hand-pulled noodles and knife-sliced noodles in house. We also serve varieties of crafted dimsums, including dumplings, wontons, beef rolls, potato rolls, and chive pockets.We feature dishes influenced by the flavours, ingredients, and cooking methods of Northern China. With hand-crafted noodles & unique regional specialties we hope to exhibit the diversity of Chinese cuisine. We strive for flavours that are not only robust and mouth tingling but also fragrant and savoury. We hope you enjoy your meal!
